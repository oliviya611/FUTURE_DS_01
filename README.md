# 📊 Business Sales Performance Analytics

## 🎯 Objective
Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, high-value categories, and regional performance. The goal is to generate actionable insights that support business growth and better decision-making.

---

## 📁 Dataset
Superstore Sales Dataset (Kaggle)  
Includes data on sales, profit, products, categories, and regions.

---

## 🔍 Analysis Areas
- Revenue Trends  
- Top-Selling Products  
- Category Performance  
- Regional Performance  
- Profit Analysis  

---

## 📈 Key Results
- Sales fluctuate over time, indicating seasonal demand patterns  
- A small number of products contribute significantly to total revenue  
- The Technology category generates the highest sales  
- The West region shows the strongest performance  
- Some categories and regions underperform compared to others  

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Increase investment in high-performing categories like Technology
